No 1 Bio

Born in Miami, Florida but raised in Fairbanks, Alaska. After I graduated high school, I attended the University of Alaska Fairbanks in pursuit of a Civil Engineering degree. Two years in, I took an opportunity to leave Alaska and move to Central Florida. I quickly started a business repairing smartphones and durning my down time I started to learn how to write code. I immediately loved it and decided I want to make a career out of it.

That's when I discovered App Academy and decided to apply. I got accepted and what followed was the most intense 12 week learning experience of my life. I learned so much but I'm determined to keep learning and challenging myself.
